## Style Guidelines for All Images:
- **Mood**: Optimistic, hopeful, achievable (not dystopian or too far-fetched)
- **Color Palette**: Warm golds, clean blues, natural greens (solarpunk aesthetic)
- **Lighting**: Natural, bright, golden hour preferred
- **Diversity**: Always show diverse age, ethnicity, gender representation
- **Realism**: Photorealistic style, not overly stylized or cartoon
- **Technology**: Advanced but believable, not sci-fi fantasy
- **Atmosphere**: Abundant, clean, prosperous, dignified

## Aspect Ratios:
- Hero images: 16:9 or 21:9
- Section headers: 16:9
- Inline illustrations: 16:9 or 4:3
- Infographics: 16:9 or square

## Additional Notes:
- Avoid cryptocurrency clichés (Bitcoin coins raining from sky, etc.)
- Show technology serving humans, not replacing them
- Emphasize human dignity and freedom
- Balance futuristic elements with familiar, achievable technology
- Show real people in real situations, not staged corporate photos
