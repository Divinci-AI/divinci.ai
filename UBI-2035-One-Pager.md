## Critical Notes

### Defense Accountability

We must be clear: **national defense is paramount**. However, unacknowledged special access programs (USAPs) operating outside Congressional oversight must be brought under proper civilian control. This isn't about weakening defense—it's about restoring constitutional governance while modernizing for 21st-century threats.

### Tax Fairness

**Closing loopholes isn't raising taxes**—it's ensuring the wealthy pay what they already owe:

- Partnership basis shifting: $100B over 10 years
- Billionaire borrowing loophole: $102B over 10 years
- Carried interest: $15-20B over 10 years
- Corporate stock buyback tax increase: Billions annually

## Proven Precedents

UBI isn't theoretical—it works:

- **Alaska Permanent Fund:** 40+ years paying $1,000-2,072 annually. No reduction in employment, 20% child poverty decrease.
- **Finland Pilot:** Improved mental health, increased entrepreneurship, maintained employment.
- **Kenya Trial (GiveDirectly):** Largest UBI trial (20,000+ recipients) shows economic multiplier effects, increased investment.
- **Stockton, CA:** $500/month improved employment, financial stability, and health outcomes.
