About Divinci AI

Divinci AI is dedicated to accelerating human discovery through innovative AI solutions. Our mission is to empower organizations with advanced AI tools that enhance learning, creativity, and decision-making, fostering a future where technology amplifies human potential.

Our Summer 2025 AI Internship Program offers USC students a unique opportunity to work on real-world AI projects with industry experts. This 10-week, unpaid internship provides valuable experience, mentorship, and deliverables for academic credit or university presentation.

Interns will work primarily remotely, with optional in-person meetings in Santa Monica, CA for those able to attend. All projects are designed to provide meaningful contributions to both your learning and our technology.

Internship Projects

As an intern, you'll work on one of the following innovative AI projects:

AI Interpretability

Explore large language models by replicating Anthropic's circuit tracing research and developing interpretability tools using SHAP and Hugging Face.

This project will help you understand the inner workings of sophisticated language models and contribute to the growing field of AI explainability.

Model Context Protocol (MCP) Integration

Build Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) APIs with MCP to connect AI systems to external data, leveraging open-source Python SDKs.

Learn how to enhance AI capabilities by providing relevant context from external sources, a critical component in enterprise AI applications.

Customer-Facing API Development

Transform internal RAG and fine-tuning APIs into secure, customer-ready systems with webhooks, implementing OAuth 2.0 and industry-standard security.

Gain experience in API security, authentication protocols, and creating developer-friendly interfaces for enterprise customers.

Who We're Looking For

We seek motivated USC undergraduate or graduate students in computer science, data science, engineering, or related fields with:

  • Basic proficiency in Python or TypeScript and familiarity with Git
  • A passion for AI, machine learning, or API development
  • Strong problem-solving skills and eagerness to learn in a collaborative, mentor-driven environment
  • Interest in contributing to impactful projects with real-world applications
