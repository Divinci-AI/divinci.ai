Introduction to Divinci AI

What is Divinci AI?

Divinci AI is a platform that empowers organizations to create custom AI solutions without requiring specialized machine learning expertise. Our platform makes advanced AI capabilities accessible through intuitive interfaces and APIs.

With Divinci AI, you can build AI applications that leverage your organization's unique data and knowledge, enhancing learning, creativity, and decision-making across your business.

Key Features

AutoRAG

Automate the complex process of implementing Retrieval-Augmented Generation systems, allowing your AI to access and reason over your organization's proprietary documents and knowledge.

Learn more about AutoRAG

Quality Assurance for LLMs

Comprehensive tools to test, monitor, and improve the performance of your AI models, ensuring they meet your organization's standards for accuracy, relevance, and consistency.

Explore QA capabilities

Release Cycle Management

Tools and workflows to manage the development, testing, and deployment of AI models in a controlled, repeatable way that integrates with your existing development processes.

Discover release management
