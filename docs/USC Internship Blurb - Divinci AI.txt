**Summer 2025 AI Internship with Divinci AI, Inc.**

Divinci AI, Inc. is excited to offer a Summer 2025 AI Internship Program for USC students passionate about advancing artificial intelligence. This 10-week, unpaid internship (June 2 – August 8, 2025) provides hands-on experience in cutting-edge AI projects, mentorship from industry experts, and deliverables for academic credit or university presentation. Interns will work remotely (with optional in-person meetings in Santa Monica, CA) on one of three innovative projects:

- **AI Interpretability**: Explore large language models by replicating Anthropic’s circuit tracing research and developing interpretability tools using SHAP and Hugging Face.
- **Model Context Protocol (MCP) Integration**: Build Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) APIs with MCP to connect AI systems to external data, leveraging open-source Python SDKs.
- **Customer-Facing API Development**: Transform internal RAG and fine-tuning APIs into secure, customer-ready systems with webhooks, implementing OAuth 2.0 and industry-standard security.

**About Divinci AI, Inc.**: Divinci AI is dedicated to accelerating human discovery through innovative AI solutions. Our mission is to empower organizations with advanced AI tools that enhance learning, creativity, and decision-making, fostering a future where technology amplifies human potential.

**Who We’re Looking For**: We seek motivated USC undergraduate or graduate students in computer science, data science, engineering, or related fields with:
- Basic proficiency in Python or TypeScript and familiarity with Git.
- A passion for AI, machine learning, or API development.
- Strong problem-solving skills and eagerness to learn in a collaborative, mentor-driven environment.
- Interest in contributing to impactful projects with real-world applications.

This internship offers USC students a unique opportunity to gain practical AI experience, build portfolios with GitHub deliverables, and present their work academically. To learn more or apply, contact Michael D. Mooring at [mike@divinci.app] by May 12, 2025. Join us to shape the future of AI with Divinci AI!
