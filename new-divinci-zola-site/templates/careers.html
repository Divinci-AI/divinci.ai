Our Culture

At Divinci AI, we're building a collaborative, innovative, and inclusive workplace where everyone can thrive. Our culture is defined by our shared values and commitment to creating AI technology that empowers humans rather than replacing them.

Innovation

We encourage bold thinking and creative problem-solving. We're not afraid to challenge the status quo and push the boundaries of what's possible with AI.

Collaboration

We believe the best work happens when diverse perspectives come together. We foster a collaborative environment where ideas are shared openly and everyone's voice is heard.

Responsibility

We're committed to developing AI responsibly, with strong ethical guidelines and a focus on creating technology that benefits humanity and respects individual privacy and agency.

Impact

We're driven by the opportunity to make a meaningful impact on how businesses leverage AI. Every team member plays a crucial role in bringing this vision to life.

Benefits & Perks

We believe in taking care of our team. Here are some of the benefits and perks we offer to Divinci AI employees:

Comprehensive Healthcare

We offer top-tier medical, dental, and vision coverage for employees and their dependents, with the majority of premiums covered by Divinci AI.

Competitive Compensation

We offer competitive salaries, equity options, and a 401(k) plan with employer matching to ensure you share in the company's success.

Flexible Work

We support remote work with the option for in-office collaboration at our Santa Monica headquarters, giving you the flexibility to work where you're most productive.

Generous Time Off

We offer unlimited PTO, company holidays, and paid parental leave because we believe in work-life balance and taking time to recharge.

Learning & Development

We provide an annual learning stipend for conferences, courses, and books, plus regular opportunities for professional development.

Team Events

Regular team outings, social events, and company retreats help build strong relationships and a cohesive team culture.

Our Interview Process

We've designed our interview process to be thorough but respectful of your time. Here's what you can expect:

1

Application Review

We review your application and portfolio to understand your background and interests.

2

Initial Conversation

A brief call with our recruiting team to discuss the role and answer any questions you have.

3

Technical Assessment

Depending on the role, this may include a coding challenge, design exercise, or case study.

4

Team Interviews

Meet with team members to discuss technical topics and learn about our culture.

5

Final Interview

A conversation with leadership about vision, goals, and how you'd contribute to our mission.

6

Offer

If it's a mutual fit, we'll extend an offer and help you transition to the team.
