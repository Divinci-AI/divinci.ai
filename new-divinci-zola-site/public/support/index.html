Frequently Asked Questions

How does Divinci AI differ from other AI platforms?

Divinci AI stands out through its customizable AI creation process, allowing users to build specialized AI models without technical expertise. Our platform focuses on domain-specific knowledge integration, ethical AI development, and seamless multi-platform deployment.

Unlike general-purpose AI systems, Divinci creates models that are deeply knowledgeable about your specific domain, ensuring more accurate and relevant responses for your use case.

What types of data can I use to train my AI?

Divinci AI supports various data types for training, including:

  • Text documents (PDF, DOCX, TXT)
  • FAQs and knowledge bases
  • Product catalogs and specifications
  • Process documentation and guidelines
  • Structured datasets (CSV, JSON)
  • Website content (via crawling)

All training data must comply with our content policies and should not include confidential or sensitive personal information unless you&#x27;re using our secure enterprise option with privacy guarantees.

How long does the AI training process take?

Training duration depends on the volume and complexity of your data:

  • Basic models (under 100MB of data): 30-60 minutes
  • Standard models (100MB-1GB): 2-4 hours
  • Comprehensive models (1GB-5GB): 4-12 hours
  • Enterprise models (5GB+): Custom timeframes

Our platform provides real-time progress updates and notifications when training is complete. You can continue using the platform during training, and incremental training updates typically take less time than initial training.

Can I integrate my Divinci AI with existing systems?

Yes, Divinci AI provides multiple integration options:

  • API Integration: RESTful API for custom applications
  • Web Widgets: Embeddable chat interfaces for websites
  • Mobile SDK: Native integration for iOS and Android apps
  • Pre-built Connectors: For platforms like Slack, Microsoft Teams, Zendesk, and Salesforce
  • Webhook Support: For event-based integration flows

Our Developer Hub provides detailed documentation, code samples, and integration guides for all supported platforms.

How secure is my data with Divinci AI?

Divinci AI implements robust security measures including:

  • Data Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Role-Based Access: Granular permission controls
  • SOC 2 Compliance: Regular security audits
  • Data Isolation: Your training data is used only for your AI models
  • Private Cloud Options: For enterprise customers with enhanced security requirements

For more information, please review our Privacy Policy and Security Documentation.

What kind of support do you offer?

We offer tiered support options to match your needs:

  • Standard Support: Email support with 24-hour response time (included with Basic plan)
  • Professional Support: Priority email and chat support with 8-hour response time (included with Professional plan)
  • Enterprise Support: 24/7 support with dedicated account manager, 1-4 hour response time, and custom SLAs (included with Enterprise plan)

All customers have access to our documentation, knowledge base, community forums, and regular webinars.
