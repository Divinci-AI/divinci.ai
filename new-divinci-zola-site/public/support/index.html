Frequently Asked Questions

    <div class="faq-container">
        <div class="faq-item">
            <div class="faq-question">How does Divinci AI differ from other AI platforms?</div>
            <div class="faq-answer">
                <p>Divinci AI stands out through its customizable AI creation process, allowing users to build specialized AI models without technical expertise. Our platform focuses on domain-specific knowledge integration, ethical AI development, and seamless multi-platform deployment.</p>
                <p>Unlike general-purpose AI systems, Divinci creates models that are deeply knowledgeable about your specific domain, ensuring more accurate and relevant responses for your use case.</p>
            </div>
        </div>
        
        <div class="faq-item">
            <div class="faq-question">What types of data can I use to train my AI?</div>
            <div class="faq-answer">
                <p>Divinci AI supports various data types for training, including:</p>
                <ul>
                    <li>Text documents (PDF, DOCX, TXT)</li>
                    <li>FAQs and knowledge bases</li>
                    <li>Product catalogs and specifications</li>
                    <li>Process documentation and guidelines</li>
                    <li>Structured datasets (CSV, JSON)</li>
                    <li>Website content (via crawling)</li>
                </ul>
                <p>All training data must comply with our content policies and should not include confidential or sensitive personal information unless you're using our secure enterprise option with privacy guarantees.</p>
            </div>
        </div>
        
        <div class="faq-item">
            <div class="faq-question">How long does the AI training process take?</div>
            <div class="faq-answer">
                <p>Training duration depends on the volume and complexity of your data:</p>
                <ul>
                    <li><strong>Basic models (under 100MB of data):</strong> 30-60 minutes</li>
                    <li><strong>Standard models (100MB-1GB):</strong> 2-4 hours</li>
                    <li><strong>Comprehensive models (1GB-5GB):</strong> 4-12 hours</li>
                    <li><strong>Enterprise models (5GB+):</strong> Custom timeframes</li>
                </ul>
                <p>Our platform provides real-time progress updates and notifications when training is complete. You can continue using the platform during training, and incremental training updates typically take less time than initial training.</p>
            </div>
        </div>
        
        <div class="faq-item">
            <div class="faq-question">Can I integrate my Divinci AI with existing systems?</div>
            <div class="faq-answer">
                <p>Yes, Divinci AI provides multiple integration options:</p>
                <ul>
                    <li><strong>API Integration:</strong> RESTful API for custom applications</li>
                    <li><strong>Web Widgets:</strong> Embeddable chat interfaces for websites</li>
                    <li><strong>Mobile SDK:</strong> Native integration for iOS and Android apps</li>
                    <li><strong>Pre-built Connectors:</strong> For platforms like Slack, Microsoft Teams, Zendesk, and Salesforce</li>
                    <li><strong>Webhook Support:</strong> For event-based integration flows</li>
                </ul>
                <p>Our Developer Hub provides detailed documentation, code samples, and integration guides for all supported platforms.</p>
            </div>
        </div>
        
        <div class="faq-item">
            <div class="faq-question">How secure is my data with Divinci AI?</div>
            <div class="faq-answer">
                <p>Divinci AI implements robust security measures including:</p>
                <ul>
                    <li><strong>Data Encryption:</strong> All data is encrypted in transit and at rest</li>
                    <li><strong>Role-Based Access:</strong> Granular permission controls</li>
                    <li><strong>SOC 2 Compliance:</strong> Regular security audits</li>
                    <li><strong>Data Isolation:</strong> Your training data is used only for your AI models</li>
                    <li><strong>Private Cloud Options:</strong> For enterprise customers with enhanced security requirements</li>
                </ul>
                <p>For more information, please review our <a href="/privacy-policy">Privacy Policy</a> and Security Documentation.</p>
            </div>
        </div>
        
        <div class="faq-item">
            <div class="faq-question">What kind of support do you offer?</div>
            <div class="faq-answer">
                <p>We offer tiered support options to match your needs:</p>
                <ul>
                    <li><strong>Standard Support:</strong> Email support with 24-hour response time (included with Basic plan)</li>
                    <li><strong>Professional Support:</strong> Priority email and chat support with 8-hour response time (included with Professional plan)</li>
                    <li><strong>Enterprise Support:</strong> 24/7 support with dedicated account manager, 1-4 hour response time, and custom SLAs (included with Enterprise plan)</li>
                </ul>
                <p>All customers have access to our documentation, knowledge base, community forums, and regular webinars.</p>
            </div>
        </div>
    </div>
</div>
