## Usage Notes:

1. **Duration**: Keep all animations 5-10 seconds for optimal engagement
2. **Aspect Ratio**: Request 16:9 (1920x1080) for web use
3. **Style Consistency**: Maintain the Renaissance meets modern tech aesthetic throughout
4. **Brand Colors**: Incorporate Divinci AI's tan/ochre (#F8F4F0), deep green (#1E3A2B), and gold accents
5. **Export Settings**: Request MP4 output, then convert to WebM using our ffmpeg pipeline
6. **Optimization**: Always convert to WebM and upload both formats to R2 for optimal delivery
