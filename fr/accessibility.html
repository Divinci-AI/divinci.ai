Accessibility Statement

Last Updated: May 2, 2025

Our Commitment to Accessibility

Divinci AI is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and applying the relevant accessibility standards.

Conformance Status

The Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) defines requirements for designers and developers to improve accessibility for people with disabilities. It defines three levels of conformance: Level A, Level AA, and Level AAA.

Divinci AI is partially conformant with WCAG 2.1 level AA. Partially conformant means that some parts of the content do not fully conform to the accessibility standard.

Accessibility Features

Our website includes the following accessibility features:

  • Keyboard navigation support
  • Semantic HTML structure
  • Alt text for images
  • ARIA landmarks and roles
  • Color contrast compliance
  • Skip navigation links
  • Resizable text
  • Consistent navigation

Feedback

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of the Divinci AI website. Please let us know if you encounter accessibility barriers:

  • Email: accessibility@divinci.ai
  • We aim to respond to feedback within 5 business days.

Technical Specifications

Accessibility of the Divinci AI website relies on the following technologies to work with the particular combination of web browser and any assistive technologies or plugins installed on your computer:

  • HTML
  • WAI-ARIA
  • CSS
  • JavaScript

These technologies are relied upon for conformance with the accessibility standards used.

Assessment Approach

Divinci AI assessed the accessibility of our website by the following approaches:

  • Self-evaluation
  • Automated testing using accessibility evaluation tools
  • Manual keyboard testing
  • Screen reader testing

Compatibility with Browsers and Assistive Technology

The Divinci AI website is designed to be compatible with the following assistive technologies:

  • Screen readers (including NVDA, JAWS, and VoiceOver)
  • Screen magnifiers
  • Voice recognition software
  • Keyboard-only navigation

The website is compatible with recent versions of major browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge.

Limitations and Alternatives

Despite our best efforts to ensure accessibility of the Divinci AI website, there may be some limitations. Below is a description of known limitations and alternatives:

  • Interactive visualizations: Some of our data visualizations may be challenging for screen reader users. We provide alternative text descriptions and CSV data downloads where applicable.
  • Third-party content: Some of our pages contain third-party content that is not within our control. We endeavor to work with providers who support accessibility.

Ongoing Improvements

Divinci AI is committed to continually improving the accessibility of our website to ensure we provide equal access to all of our users. We will continue to make changes and improvements to meet WCAG guidelines as much as possible.
