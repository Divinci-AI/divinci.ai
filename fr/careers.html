Our Culture

At Divinci AI, we're building a collaborative, innovative, and inclusive workplace where everyone can thrive. Our culture is defined by our shared values and commitment to creating AI technology that empowers humans rather than replacing them.

Innovation

We encourage bold thinking and creative problem-solving. We're not afraid to challenge the status quo and push the boundaries of what's possible with AI.

Collaboration

We believe the best work happens when diverse perspectives come together. We foster a collaborative environment where ideas are shared openly and everyone's voice is heard.

Responsibility

We're committed to developing AI responsibly, with strong ethical guidelines and a focus on creating technology that benefits humanity and respects individual privacy and agency.

Impact

We're driven by the opportunity to make a meaningful impact on how businesses leverage AI. Every team member plays a crucial role in bringing this vision to life.

Our Interview Process

We've designed our interview process to be thorough yet respectful of your time. Here's what you can expect when applying to Divinci AI:

1

Application Review

Our recruiting team reviews your application and assesses your experience against the role requirements. We aim to respond to all applications within one week.

2

Initial Conversation

A 30-minute call with a recruiter to discuss your background, the role, and determine mutual fit. We'll answer any questions you have about Divinci AI and the position.

3

Technical Assessment

Depending on the role, you'll complete a take-home assignment or a live technical interview. We design our assessments to be relevant to the actual work you'd do at Divinci AI.

4

Team Interviews

A series of interviews with team members and cross-functional partners. We'll dive deeper into your experience and assess team fit while giving you the opportunity to meet potential colleagues.

5

Final Interview

A conversation with a senior leader to discuss your career goals and how you can contribute to Divinci AI's mission. This is also your chance to ask any remaining questions about the company and role.

6

Offer

If there's mutual interest in moving forward, we'll extend an offer and discuss compensation, benefits, and start date. We're committed to providing a competitive package that recognizes your value.
